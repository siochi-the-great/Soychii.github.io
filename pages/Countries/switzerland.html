  • I want to visit Switzerland to experience its breathtaking winter season and possibly own a home there someday, as the peaceful environment is i ncredibly relaxing. The stunning landscapes, especially the Alps, make it a perfect destination for exploring snow-covered mountains and enjoying winter activities. I also want to visit Lucerne, known for its picturesque lakes, charming old town, and scenic mountain views. Switzerland’s natural beauty and serene atmosphere make it an ideal place to visit and even settle in the future.

How to get there

  • To get to the Swiss Alps and Lucerne, the best option is to fly into Zurich Airport (ZRH), Switzerland’s largest international airport. From there, Switzerland’s efficient train system makes traveling easy. To reach Lucerne, you can take a direct train from Zurich, which takes about an hour. For the Alps, destinations like Zermatt, Interlaken, or Jungfraujoch can be reached by scenic train routes such as the Glacier Express or the Bernese Oberland Railway. Switzerland’s reliable public transport, including cable cars and mountain trains, ensures a smooth journey to these breathtaking locations.
