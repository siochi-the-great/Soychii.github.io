Germany

The Reason why i want to visit this place

  • I want to visit Germany, especially Berlin and Frankfurt, to experience their culture and rich history, particularly their significance during World War II. In Berlin, I’d explore landmarks like the Brandenburg Gate, the Berlin Wall Memorial, and the Topography of Terror, which offer insights into the city's past. Frankfurt, heavily bombed during the war, features a fascinating mix of historic and modern architecture, such as the Römer and its reconstructed Old Town. Visiting these cities would provide a deeper understanding of Germany’s history and resilience.

How to get there

  • Traveling to Germany is easiest by air, with major international airports like Berlin Brandenburg Airport (BER) and Frankfurt Airport (FRA) serving as key entry points. Many airlines offer direct and connecting flights from around the world, so booking in advance can help with costs. Once in Germany, the country’s efficient transportation system, including high-speed ICE trains, subways, and buses, makes traveling between cities like Berlin and Frankfurt easy. Visitors should also ensure they have a valid passport and any required visas for a smooth entry.
